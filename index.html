Welcome To Carnival

Carnival Restaurant & Banquet is the perfect destination for fine dining and grand celebrations, offering a blend of luxury, comfort, and impeccable service. Whether you're planning a wedding, reception, corporate event, or family gathering, our spacious banquet hall, with a capacity of 100 to 800 guests, provides a sophisticated ambiance to make your occasion truly special. Designed with elegance and equipped with modern amenities, our venue ensures a seamless experience for you and your guests. Alongside our banquet services, our multi-cuisine restaurant offers an exquisite selection of Indian, Chinese, Continental, and other global flavors, crafted by expert chefs to deliver a delightful dining experience. From intimate dinners to grand feasts, every meal at Carnival is prepared with passion and precision. Located in the Kalol, Gandhinagar, our venue is ideal for those seeking an extraordinary experience with stunning views, a vivid ambience, and delicious food. Whether you're hosting a special event or simply looking for a place to enjoy a memorable meal, Carnival Restaurant & Banquet welcomes you to indulge in elegance, taste, and celebration. Book your event today and create unforgettable memories with us!
