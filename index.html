Welcome To Carnival

Carnival Restaurant & Banquet is the ideal destination in Kalol, Gandhinagar for unforgettable dining and grand celebrations. With a spacious banquet hall for 100 to 600 guests and a multi-cuisine restaurant offering Indian, Chinese, and Continental flavors, Carnival blends elegance, comfort, and taste. Designed with elegance and equipped with modern amenities, our venue ensures a seamless experience for you and your guests. Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or family gathering, every moment here is crafted with care.
